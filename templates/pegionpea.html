Planting and Care
Pegionpea

Use a balanced fertilizer every three months until the tree begins to bear fruit, and apply nitrogen annually once the tree is mature. Watering is done early in the morning if the soil is dry until the plants get established. Prune the plant after harvesting the fruits.
