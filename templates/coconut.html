Planting and Care
Coconut

First, water the coconut tree frequently. As long as the soil drains well, you really cant water it too often. If you decide to repot your coconut tree, remember to add sand or vermiculite to the new soil to keep the water draining well. Second, growing coconut palms are heavy feeders that require regular, complete fertilizer. Look for a fertilizer that provides both the basic nutrients plus trace nutrients like boron, manganese and magnesium.
